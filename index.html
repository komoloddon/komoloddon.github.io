I am a photographer and filmmaker based in Bremen. With over four years of experience across photography and video production, I capture people, animals, and sports with a focus on authentic, purposeful visuals.

After studying engineering, I made the deliberate decision to leave and pursue media full-time — a choice that reflects the depth of my commitment to the craft. My work has been primarily in documentary production, and I am expanding into narrative film and beyond.

I am actively looking for work and new experiences that will push my craft to the next level and open doors to bigger, more ambitious projects.
